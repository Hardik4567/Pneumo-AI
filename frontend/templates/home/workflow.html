AI Workflow

How PneumoAI Works

Our automated pipeline processes chest X-rays through rigorous verification, optimization, and deep learning layers to supply clinical staff with swift, explainable diagnostics.

01 Input

Upload Chest X-ray

Upload a standard digital PNG or JPEG posterior-anterior chest X-ray securely utilizing the drag-and-drop web dashboard.

02 Validation

Image Validation

The gateway system validates image integrity, file configurations, minimum spatial resolution, and compliance parameters.

03 Preparation

Preprocessing

Normalizes matrices, adjusts contrast variables, and resizes pixel tensors to conform to fixed neural network inputs.

04 Inference

AI Prediction

DenseNet121 evaluates structural densities to classify conditions and output target probability confidence vectors.

05 Explainability

Grad-CAM Explainability

Generates attention heatmaps by extracting gradients from final feature maps, projecting opacity shifts over opacity targets.

06 Analysis

Medical Analysis

Aggregates diagnostic insights into structural evaluation metrics ready for clinical review and decision optimization.

07 Output

Download Report

Generates complete metadata reports, localization overlays, and provides accessible, downloadable PDF clinical logs.
